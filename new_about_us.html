Project Summary


Project Discription

Our project will create a web database application for Multi-Disc to market and sell Music CDs online. This new selling platform will allow a company to engage in online markets creating increased product exposure through search engine listing and possible link advertising.

Executive Summary




Database of CDs available in store is categorized by Genre, Type (pop, rock, etc), identified by track name, artist name , album name and all information is stored into an online database. Information from the database is extracted to form a web page where customers can easily browse and make purchase of CDs which they want. There will be login procedures (general validation) to ensure customer information such as delivery address and etc. The reason for carrying out the project is simple, Music is a common field of interest many of us share and therefore it was a suitable choice as all the members of the group can provide their own insight when creating the system. This system can be applied in many Music stores that are interested in providing their customers with the option to purchase their goods online. By the end of the assigned time the sign of a successfully completed project would be the successful creation of a music system that obtains the data from a dynamically populated database for users to search and purchase music.
